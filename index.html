The Darkkkkkkk Crystal

Help the Nobel Gelfling defeat the Evil Skeksis!!


The Skeksis call a random number at the start of the game, to unlock the Dark Crystal!

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specifc amount of points to your score.

You help the Gelfling deafeat the Skesis by matching your total score to the random number value of the Dark Crysal.

The value of each of the 4 color crystals is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of every crystal!
